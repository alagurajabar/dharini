    Research Publications

    Research is a cornerstone of my professional life. I have authored a comprehensive review titled Unraveling the Complex Interplay: COVID-19 and Heart Failure. I have co-authored a review on the role of diabetes mellitus in the development and progression of atrial fibrillation, published in Human Journals. Additionally, I have several articles under peer review on topics ranging from cardiac sarcoidosis to the interplay between COVID-19 and heart failure.
