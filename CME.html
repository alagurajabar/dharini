    PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

    In my pursuit of professional improvement, continuous learning and taking part in medical activities that push my boundaries of education have been fundamental pillars. Throughout my career, I have actively sought opportunities to enhance my knowledge and skills, believing that the journey of education is perpetual. Engaging in professional competitions, continued medical education (CME) programs, and short courses has not only broadened my understanding of various medical disciplines but also allowed me to stay abreast of the latest advancements in the field. It has also enabled me to meet veteran doctors, professionals, and peers in the field of medicine who have inspired and improved my perspectives. These experiences have been instrumental in honing my clinical acumen, fostering innovation, and driving my commitment to become capable of providing the highest quality of care.


    This section outlines my participation in professional development activities, showcasing my participation in academic competitions, CME activities, and specialized short courses, all of which have significantly contributed to my growth as a healthcare professional.
